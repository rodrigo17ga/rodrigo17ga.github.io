The Native Tongues

The Native Tongues is a collective of late 1980s and early 1990s hip-hop artists known for their positive-minded, good-natured Afrocentric lyrics, and for pioneering the use of eclectic sampling and later jazz-influenced beats. Its principal members are listed below

A Tribe Called Quest

De La Soul

Jungle Brothers

Kool DJ Red Alert

Lucien Revolucien

Monie Love

Queen Latifah

Black Sheep

Chi-Ali

Fu-Schnickens
